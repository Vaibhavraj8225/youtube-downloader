### Usage
poweshell/bash
python3 downloader.py

### GUI Controls
-paste the link of the vedio or playlist in the textarea.
-Select the quality for your video.
-Enter the download location in the input field for save to or select folder using browse button.
-Click on download button.

### To download audio only.
-check the audio only chaeckbox.

### Cancel Donload.
-click on canccel download button to cancel the download.
